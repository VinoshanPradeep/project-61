About

Jai Hemant "Tiger Shroff" was born in 2 March 1990.Tiger Shroff is an Indian actor, singer and mixed martial artist. One of the highest paid actors in India, he is known for his dancing skills and work in Hindi-language action films. The son of actor Jackie Shroff and producer Ayesha Dutt, he made his film debut with the 2014 romantic action film Heropanti.Shroff is best known for his successful action films Baaghi (2016) and its sequel Baaghi 2(2018) and War (2019), which ranks as one of the highest grossing Indian films of all time. He has also featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list since 2018.
